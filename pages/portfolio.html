Projects

  • Smart Curtain

    Developed a smart curtain that automatically opens and closes based on room temperature and incoming light. Smart curtain can also be controlled manually with a button and has been proven effective in reducing room temperature by 2–3°C.

  • Mini Keyboard Controller

    A mini keyboard designed for various simple functions such as controlling media players, presentations, switching apps, and more. This keyboard is powered by an Atmega328p microcontroller programmed in C.

  • Digital Drum

    An ESP32-S3-based digital drum capable of producing drum sounds. Equipped with a piezo sensor to detect hit strength, allowing for dynamic sound variation.
